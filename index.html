Welcome To Smith & Smith Attorneys at Law

Tarrant County Trial Lawyers

Serving clients from their offices in the Ridglea area of Fort Worth, Smith & Smith focuses on personal injury and civil litigation.  The attorneys at Smith & Smith have the legal experience, knowledge, and skills to help both individual and corporate clients through all aspects of legal claims and disputes.

Smith & Smith is dedicated to handling their clients’ injury and damage claims with professionalism and integrity.  We provide high quality ethical representation that frequently leads to referrals from former clients and other attorneys.

The attorneys of Smith & Smith handle personal injury claims and trial matters throughout the DFW Metroplex, as well as the surrounding counties of Johnson, Parker, Denton, Collin, Ellis, Kaufman, and Wise.

Texas Trial Lawyers

Smith & Smith has experience in all types of personal injury cases including car and truck accidents, work injuries, premises liability, slip & fall accidents, medical malpractice, nursing home negligence, and product liability.

The lawyers at Smith & Smith offer free initial consultations. Please call our office to schedule your meeting with either Daniel Smith or Michael Smith where we can provide you with an honest assessment of your case. You may call us at 817-877-5750.

If we agree to accept your personal injury case, we will work on a contingent fee basis, which means we only get paid if you do.  If we are unable to obtain compensation for you, you will not owe us anything.
